My Approach

Hi, I’m Jon. For over 40 years I’ve worked with people all over the world – from airlines and hospitality to education, business and leadership. What I’ve learned is simple: you already have the strength you need. Sometimes you just need the right space, the right questions, and the right support to bring it out.

With me, you won’t get abstract theories or corporate buzzwords. You’ll get real conversations, reflection, and action – all built around your own story and the four life pillars I work with: Body • Mind • Heart • Spirit
